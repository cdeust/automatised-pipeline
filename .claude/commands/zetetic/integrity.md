# Quick Integrity Audit

Fast integrity check on the current work. Feynman-style: what could invalidate this?

## Instructions

1. Look at the recent work in this session (recent commits, staged changes, or $ARGUMENTS if provided).

2. For the main claim/result/change, list 3-5 things that could invalidate it, ranked by damage.

3. For each: is there evidence for or against it? Has it been checked?

4. Self-deception check: is the team invested in this being true? What procedural check would catch bias?

5. Cargo-cult check: is any procedure being followed "because successful teams do it" without the causal mechanism?

6. Present as:
   ```
   Quick Integrity Audit
   
   What could invalidate this:
   1. [highest damage] — checked: [yes/no]
   2. ...
   
   Self-deception risk: [high/medium/low]
   Cargo cult candidates: [list or none]
   ```
